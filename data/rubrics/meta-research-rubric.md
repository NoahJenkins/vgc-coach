# Meta Research Rubric

Score each response on:

- `Format correctness`
  - confirms active regulation and mechanics accurately
- `Source discipline`
  - uses official sources for legality and live sources for current meta claims
- `Freshness handling`
  - treats current claims as time-sensitive and labels weak/conflicting data
- `Meta usefulness`
  - identifies real top threats, teams, and strategic trends
- `Anti-meta value`
  - gives concrete openings instead of generic “be different” advice
- `Inference honesty`
  - distinguishes sourced facts from interpretation

Failure triggers:

- inactive mechanics presented as active
- stale SV-era assumptions presented as current Champions truth
- invented usage figures or unsupported certainty

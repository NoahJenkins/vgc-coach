# Good Example 1

Request:

`Audit this rain team for why it keeps losing to Incineroar balance and opposing weather control.`

Why this is good:

- starts with the highest-impact issues
- ties them to real matchup losses
- gives fixes that preserve the team's rain identity
- explains whether the problem is weather dependency, positioning, or preserve logic instead of saying the team is "bad into balance"
- ends with residual risk rather than pretending the team is fixed everywhere

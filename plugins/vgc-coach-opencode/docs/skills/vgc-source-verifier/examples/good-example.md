# Good Example 1

Request:

`Check whether this draft claim is really supported: "Rain is the clear best team style in Regulation M-A because ChampionsMeta lists multiple winning Pelipper teams."`

Why this is good:

- turns the sentence into an auditable claim
- checks whether the cited evidence proves "clear best" or something weaker
- distinguishes observed trend from overclaimed conclusion
- tells the next writer what can still be said safely

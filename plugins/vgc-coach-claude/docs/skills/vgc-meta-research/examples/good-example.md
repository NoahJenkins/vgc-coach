# Good Example 1

Request:

`What is the current Pokemon Champions Reg M-A meta and what are two anti-meta openings?`

Good output shape:

## Format Check

Confirms that Regulation M-A is active on April 18, 2026, and that Mega Evolution is active while Terastallization is not listed as active in the official regulation announcement.

## Meta Snapshot

Summarizes the current top layer of the format using live sources, for example:

- `Incineroar`, `Sneasler`, `Garchomp`, `Sinistcha`, `Kingambit`, `Basculegion`, `Whimsicott`
- weather and tempo shells are prominent
- data is still early-format and moving quickly

## Popular Teams and Cores

Calls out things like:

- `Pelipper + Basculegion`
- `Archaludon + Pelipper`
- `Tyranitar + Excadrill`
- `Torkoal + Venusaur`

## Strategic Trends

Explains that the field is leaning on:

- weather compression
- strong pivot/tempo glue like `Incineroar`
- speed control through Tailwind, weather speed, or anti-speed shells

## Anti-Meta Openings

Gives concrete takeaways such as:

- anti-rain structures that punish `Pelipper + Basculegion`
- anti-Incin balance builds that exploit Dark-type overcentralization or tempo assumptions

## Sources

Lists official rules plus live community sources.

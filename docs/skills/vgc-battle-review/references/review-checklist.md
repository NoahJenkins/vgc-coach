# Review Checklist

## Intent Check

Before criticizing a turn, ask:

- What was the player's likely plan?
- What information did they have at the time?
- What needed to be preserved for the endgame?

## Decision-Point Check

Focus on:

- lead and preview mistakes
- preserve or sacrifice errors
- missed positioning lines
- speed-control, weather, or board-state sequencing errors
- assumptions that were unreasonable or unsupported

## Variance Check

Separate:

- true misplays
- acceptable but losing lines
- bad outcomes caused mainly by low rolls, misses, or hidden information

## Better-Line Rules

- give alternative lines the player could realistically have found
- explain why the line was stronger on that board
- prefer one or two high-value alternatives over a pile of hindsight branches

## Avoid These Failure Modes

- blaming every loss on execution
- pretending perfect information was available
- vague coaching with no exact decision point
- review output that never changes future prep

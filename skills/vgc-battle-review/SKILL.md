# VGC Battle Review

## Purpose

Use this skill to review:

- replay summaries
- logs
- turn-by-turn notes
- post-game self-reflections

## Output Contract

Always return:

1. key turning points
2. actual mistakes vs bad variance
3. stronger alternative lines
4. what to practice next

## Rules

- do not call every loss a misplay
- identify the decision point, not just the bad result
- tie review output back to future prep

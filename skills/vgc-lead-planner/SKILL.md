# VGC Lead Planner

## Purpose

Use this skill to convert a team into actual opening plans.

## Inputs

- full team
- target matchups or archetypes
- optional open team sheet assumptions

## Output Contract

Always return:

1. default lead
2. matchup-specific leads
3. preserve targets
4. first-turn priorities
5. common traps

## Rules

- avoid fake certainty
- show how plans differ by matchup
- prioritize useful practical lines over exhaustive trees
